1. K.'s Introduction to the Group

K. was introduced to a prestigious group consisting of judges, public prosecutors, and lawyers through a lawyer who represented the bank where K. worked. This group was known for its serious professional conversations, and K. found it an honor to be part of such an assembly. His initial inclusion allowed him to learn significantly and establish valuable contacts, which he found beneficial for his career at the bank. K.'s business acumen was respected within the group, and he was often consulted on legal matters, despite his primary expertise being in business.

2. K.'s Growing Relationship with Public Prosecutor Hasterer

Over time, K. developed a close friendship with Public Prosecutor Hasterer, which became widely recognized within the group. This friendship led to K. being considered under Hasterer's patronage, granting him a unique and privileged position among the group. Hasterer's respect and fear within the group were significant, and his support for K. solidified K.'s standing within the group.

3. K.'s Privileged Position Among the Group

K.'s relationship with Hasterer and his own capabilities led to his elevated status within the group. He was seen as a valuable member whose opinions were highly regarded, even in matters outside his direct expertise. This privileged position was further reinforced by Hasterer's backing, making K. an integral part of the group's dynamics.

4. Hasterer's Dominance and Influence

Hasterer was known for his strong opinions and the aggressive defense of his views. His influence extended beyond professional matters, as he was also a central figure in the social interactions within the group. Hasterer's demeanor and method of engaging with others underscored his dominance and the respect he commanded among his peers.

5. K.'s Evening Visits to Hasterer's Apartment

K.'s friendship with Hasterer extended to personal invitations to Hasterer's apartment, where they would spend time discussing various matters over drinks. These visits highlighted the depth of their relationship and the mutual enjoyment they derived from each other's company. The presence of Hasterer's companion, Helene, during some of these visits added a personal dimension to their interactions.

6. The President's Recognition of K.'s Relationship with Hasterer

The president of the bank, aware of K.'s association with Hasterer, expressed surprise and interest in this relationship. This recognition by the president indicated the significance of K.'s connection with Hasterer and its potential implications for K.'s standing both within the bank and the broader professional community.
